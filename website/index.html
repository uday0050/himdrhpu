Key Features of HIM-DR³

  • Six Specialized Thematic Divisions: Seismic hazards, hydro-meteorological risks (GLOFs, cloudbursts, flash floods, droughts), community-based resilience, technological innovations, ecosystem-based solutions, and institutional governance.
  • Dedicated Infrastructure:
    • Infrastructure Testing & Research Facility for structural safety, terrain stability, and consultancy support.
    • GIS & Remote Sensing Lab for multi-hazard mapping, real-time monitoring, and spatial decision systems.
  • Multidisciplinary Collaboration: Expertise from 52+ academic departments across sciences, engineering, law, economics, and social sciences.
  • Functions: Academic programs, R&D projects, consultancy, training, simulation, community outreach, and policy advisory.

Relevance for the State

  • Hazard mapping, infrastructure testing, and climate risk assessments
  • Early warning systems & disaster simulation models
  • Capacity building & training for government officials and communities
  • Policy formulation, governance frameworks, and consultancy in disaster management

Given the frequent and complex disasters impacting Himachal Pradesh, we respectfully request that the Centre may be actively involved in State-level disaster management initiatives, consultations, and field missions.
